Common questions

How does saving work?
Picking a shade is always free. A one-time unlock lets the app remember your own coffee; a second unlock remembers a saved shade for everyone you order for. Your shades live on your device.

Do you collect my data?
No. Crema has no accounts and no analytics, and stores your shades only on your phone. See our Privacy Policy.

I bought an unlock on another phone.
Open the paywall and tap “restore purchases.” Unlocks are tied to your Apple ID.
